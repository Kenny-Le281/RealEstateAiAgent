About this home

Charming New Home on a Spacious 1-Acre Lot Welcome to this brand-new 4-bedroom, 2-bathroom home offering 1,791 sq. ft. of thoughtfully designed living space. Sitting on a level 1-acre lot, this property combines modern convenience with peaceful country living. The home features all-new appliances, a newly paved driveway with a parking pad, and construction by Clayton-recognized for building some of the most energy-efficient homes available today. With plenty of outdoor space, there's room to create your ideal retreat-add a pool, start a garden, build a workshop, or set up extra storage. Enjoy the privacy of wide open surroundings and the freedom of no HOA restrictions. The location offers the best of both worlds: tucked away from the hustle and bustle of Atlanta traffic, yet minutes from shopping, dining, the Dawsonville Outlets, schools, the University of North Georgia, Northeast Georgia Medical Center, and easy access to Hwy 400. This home qualifies for all types of financing, including USDA and FHA, making it a versatile option whether you're searching for a personal residence or an investment opportunity near UNG.
